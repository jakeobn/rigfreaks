Return & Refund Policy

Last Updated: April 25, 2025

Information Notice

PC Builder is a configuration and planning tool. We do not sell computer components directly. This policy provides general guidelines for returns and refunds of PC components purchased from retailers based on our recommendations.

1. General Information

PC Builder is a service that helps users configure compatible PC builds. We do not sell physical products directly. The components you select and purchase will be subject to the return policies of the retailer where you make your purchase.

This document provides general guidelines and best practices for returns and exchanges of PC components. For specific return policies, always refer to the retailer where you purchased your components.

2. Retailer Return Policies

When purchasing computer components based on our recommendations, we suggest checking the following details from your retailer:

  • Return window (typically 14-30 days from purchase)
  • Restocking fees (if applicable)
  • Return shipping costs
  • Condition requirements for returns (packaging, accessories, etc.)
  • Special policies for opened electronic components

3. Compatibility Issues

While our PC Builder tool performs compatibility checks on components, we cannot guarantee 100% compatibility in all scenarios due to variations in manufacturing, firmware versions, and other factors beyond our control.

If you encounter compatibility issues with components selected through our service:

  1. Contact us through our support channels for troubleshooting assistance
  2. If the issue cannot be resolved, we can provide documentation explaining the compatibility issue to support your return request with the retailer
  3. Follow the retailer's return process for components that need to be exchanged

4. Best Practices for PC Component Returns

4.1 Documentation

Keep all of the following documents for at least 90 days after purchase:

  • Original receipts and order confirmations
  • Shipping labels and tracking information
  • All packaging materials and boxes
  • Warranty cards and product documentation
4.2 Testing

Test all components as soon as possible after receiving them:

  • Verify the component matches the specifications you ordered
  • Check for any physical damage before installation
  • Document the condition of the component before installation with photos if possible
4.3 Return Preparation

If you need to return a component:

  • Repackage the item in its original packaging
  • Include all accessories, manuals, and items that came with the product
  • Remove any personal data from storage devices
  • Take photos of the packaged item before shipping
  • Get a tracking number for your return shipment

5. Warranty Information

Most PC components come with manufacturer warranties that extend beyond the retailer's return period:

  • CPUs: Typically 3 years
  • Motherboards: Typically 2-3 years
  • Graphics Cards: Typically 2-3 years
  • RAM: Often lifetime warranty or 10+ years
  • Power Supplies: Typically 5-10 years for quality units
  • Storage (SSDs, HDDs): Typically 3-5 years
  • Cases: Typically 1-2 years

For warranty claims after the retailer's return period, contact the component manufacturer directly.

6. PC Builder Premium Service Refunds

For our premium services (if applicable):

  • We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee on premium subscription services
  • Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days to the original payment method
  • To request a refund for premium services, contact our support team through the Contact Us page

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about returns or need assistance with a component issue, please contact our support team.
