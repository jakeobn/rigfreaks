Warranty & Returns Policy

Last Updated: April 26, 2025

3-Year Premium Warranty

Every EverCraft custom desktop computer comes with our comprehensive 3-year warranty covering parts and labor. We stand behind the quality and craftsmanship of our premium custom desktops.

1. Our Warranty Coverage

EverCraft provides a 3-year limited warranty on all custom desktop computers purchased through our official website. This warranty covers defects in materials and workmanship under normal use during the warranty period.

1.1 What is Covered
  • All hardware components installed by EverCraft during the initial build
  • System assembly and integration
  • Manufacturing defects
  • Hardware failures during normal use
  • Technical support for hardware-related issues
1.2 What is Not Covered
  • Software issues unrelated to hardware (including operating system problems)
  • Physical damage caused by accidents, misuse, or abuse
  • Damage from environmental factors (power surges, excessive heat, etc.)
  • Unauthorized modifications or repairs
  • Normal wear and tear
  • Consumable parts (like thermal paste)
  • Components added or replaced by the customer after purchase

2. Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your EverCraft custom desktop. If you're not entirely happy with your purchase, we're here to help.

2.1 30-Day Satisfaction Guarantee

You may return your custom desktop computer within 30 days of delivery for a refund or exchange, subject to the following conditions:

  • The system must be in like-new condition with no physical damage
  • All original packaging materials, accessories, manuals, and documentation must be included
  • Software installed on the system must be returned to its original state
  • You must remove any personal data from the system
2.2 Restocking Fee

A 15% restocking fee may apply to returned products that:

  • Show signs of use beyond initial testing
  • Are missing any original packaging, accessories, or documentation
  • Have been custom-built with non-standard component selections

The restocking fee will be deducted from your refund amount.

2.3 Return Process
  1. Contact our support team to initiate a return request
  2. Receive a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number
  3. Pack your system securely in its original packaging
  4. Ship the system to our returns center with the RMA number clearly visible on the packaging
  5. Once received and inspected, we'll process your refund or exchange

Refunds will be issued to the original payment method and typically process within 5-7 business days after we receive and inspect the returned system.

3. Warranty Service

If your EverCraft desktop experiences a hardware issue during the warranty period, we offer multiple support options:

3.1 Remote Diagnostics

Our technical support team will first attempt to diagnose and resolve issues remotely through:

  • Phone support
  • Email support
  • Remote desktop sessions (when applicable)
3.2 Repair Options

If remote support cannot resolve the issue, we offer:

  • Return-to-Base Repair: Ship your system to our service center for repair
  • Advanced Parts Replacement: For easily replaceable parts, we can ship you the replacement part with instructions
  • On-Site Service: For eligible customers, we may dispatch a technician to your location (additional fees may apply)
3.3 Shipping for Warranty Service

For warranty repairs requiring shipment to our service center:

  • EverCraft covers shipping costs both ways for warranty issues within the first year
  • After the first year, the customer is responsible for shipping to our service center, and we cover return shipping
  • We provide shipping labels and instructions for secure packaging

4. Extended Warranty Options

Enhance your protection with our extended warranty options:

  • Premium Protection Plan: Extends full coverage to 5 years
  • Accidental Damage Protection: Adds coverage for drops, spills, and other accidents
  • Express Service Upgrade: Guarantees expedited repair service and loaner systems for critical issues

Extended warranty options must be purchased at the time of your original system purchase. Contact our sales team for pricing and details.

5. Component Warranty Information

Individual components in your EverCraft custom desktop may have manufacturer warranties that extend beyond our standard 3-year warranty:

  • Power Supplies: Many premium models offer 7-10 year warranties
  • Storage Drives: SSDs typically have 3-5 year warranties
  • Memory: Often includes lifetime warranty from the manufacturer

Our warranty service covers coordinating with component manufacturers on your behalf when necessary.

6. Transfer of Warranty

The EverCraft warranty stays with the product, not the purchaser. If you sell or give your desktop to someone else, the warranty will transfer to the new owner for the remainder of the warranty period, provided you notify us of the ownership change.

To transfer warranty ownership, please contact our support team with the system serial number and the new owner's contact information.

7. Contact Warranty Support

For warranty claims, returns, or technical support:

  • Email: support@evercraft.com
  • Phone: 1-800-EVERCRAFT (1-800-383-7272)
  • Support Hours: Monday-Friday, 9am-9pm ET; Saturday, 10am-6pm ET
  • Online Support Portal: Contact Us

Please have your order number or system serial number ready when contacting support.
