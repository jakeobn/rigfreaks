Cookie Policy

RigFreaks ("we", "us", "our") uses cookies to enhance your experience while using our website. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and your choices regarding their use.

By using our website, you agree to the use of cookies as described in this policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. These files allow the website to remember your actions and preferences (such as login details, language preferences, etc.) over a period of time, so you don't have to re-enter them whenever you visit the site or browse between pages.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

We use several types of cookies on our website:

a. Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the operation of our website. Without them, certain features or functionality may not work. Examples include cookies that enable you to log in, manage your shopping cart, and complete purchases.

b. Performance Cookies

These cookies collect information about how visitors use our website, such as which pages are visited most frequently or if any error messages are received. These cookies help us improve the performance of our website. All information collected by these cookies is anonymous.

c. Functional Cookies

These cookies allow our website to remember your preferences and choices, such as language or region, to provide a more personalized experience. They can also be used to remember changes you've made to settings like font size, or to provide certain services like watching a video.

d. Targeting or Advertising Cookies

These cookies are used to deliver advertisements that are more relevant to you based on your browsing habits. They may be placed by third-party advertising partners with our consent and are used to track your activity across websites to build a profile of your interests.

3. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for various reasons, including:

  • Improving Website Functionality: Essential cookies enable basic functionality such as navigation and user authentication.
  • Tracking Performance: Performance cookies help us analyze website performance, identify issues, and improve our content and services.
  • Personalizing Content: Functional cookies allow us to tailor the content on our site to your preferences, making your experience more user-friendly.
  • Advertising and Analytics: Targeting cookies are used to serve you relevant advertisements and gather insights into your browsing behavior.

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may allow third-party services, such as Google Analytics, social media platforms (e.g., Facebook, Instagram), and advertising networks, to place cookies on our website. These third parties may use cookies to collect information about your browsing habits and interests. We do not control these cookies, and they are governed by the privacy policies of the respective third parties.

5. Your Cookie Choices

You can manage or block cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • Accept or reject cookies
  • Set your browser to alert you when a cookie is being set
  • Delete cookies already stored on your device

If you block or disable cookies, please be aware that certain features of our website may not function properly, and your user experience may be impacted.

To manage cookies, follow the instructions provided by your browser:

  • Google Chrome: Chrome Cookie Settings
  • Mozilla Firefox: Firefox Cookie Settings
  • Safari: Safari Cookie Settings
  • Microsoft Edge: Edge Cookie Settings
