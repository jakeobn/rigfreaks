Delivery Information

Custom PC Excellence, Delivered to Your Door

At RigFreaks, we are committed to delivering high-performance custom PCs that exceed your expectations. Our process is thorough, ensuring every component meets the highest standards of quality and reliability.

Order Processing and Build Time

Once you place your order, we begin by sourcing the best parts available for your custom PC. Depending on the availability of components, it can take 1-3 days for us to acquire everything needed. Afterward, our experienced technicians start assembling your system. This step typically takes 1-2 days, as we ensure every piece fits perfectly and is securely installed.

Rigorous Testing and Troubleshooting

After the build is complete, your custom PC undergoes a series of rigorous stress tests to ensure that all components are functioning at their peak performance. We test for:

  • CPU performance and stability
  • GPU load handling and temperature regulation
  • Memory stress tests to check for reliability
  • Hard drive and SSD read/write speeds
  • Overall system stability under high usage scenarios

If any issues arise during these tests, we troubleshoot immediately, replacing or adjusting parts as necessary to ensure your PC meets our high standards. This process usually takes 2-3 days, as we prioritize delivering a system that runs smoothly and reliably.

Packaging and Shipping

Once your PC passes all tests and we're confident it's in perfect working order, we carefully package it for safe delivery. We use high-quality packaging materials to ensure your PC arrives in pristine condition. Standard shipping within the UK typically takes 1-2 days, depending on the courier service.
